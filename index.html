The CGCU as a Management Group

The CGCU is more than an event-producing organisation - as some of you already know, all of ICU's societies are divided into 'Management Groups', and the CGCU is one of these groups.

It's the VPFS's main role to manage and represent all of the engineering societies and depsocs underneath this umbrella (there are 15 of them!).

First point of call for the Guilds' societies

I will be the first port of call for all of the Guilds' societies queries. I will promptly respond with answers when I know them, and if not, I will find out.

Represent the CGCU clubs on the Clubs and Societies Board

I will represent the CGCU clubs on the union's Clubs and Societies Board, ensuring that the Guilds' voice is heard in the upper levels in the union.

Regular Management Group Meetings

I will meet regularly with the CGCU societies, to understand any issues they have, and work on ways to solve them.

Daily go through eActivities finances

I will daily go through eActivities finances, to ensure that volunteers are reimbursed for club purchases in a speedy fashion. Leaving these students with prolonged financial burden is unfair.

Encourage Collaboration between Societies

I will assist and improve collaboration between societies. This could include events, available resources, and committee organisation.

Continuity in the Committee

I will provide continuity in the committee. After a year on committee, I will have experience which can be passed onto new officers, so that we learn from past decisions and improve each and every year.
